Meet the Punks

The CryptoPunks are 24x24 pixel art images, generated algorithmically. Most are punky-looking guys and girls, but there are a few rarer types mixed in: Apes, Zombies and even the odd Alien. Every punk has their own profile page that shows their attributes as well as their ownership/for-sale status.

Details and FAQ

What is the WCC?

WCC stands for Wealth Cyborg Club, a group of enthusiasts who have acquired one or more NFTs among the 10'000 in the collection.

How much will it cost to mint?

The cost of the mint depends on the pack you will take: Silver, Gold or Diamond. A WCC will cost you 0.15 ETH + gas fee. The Gold pack will cost you 0.45 ETH and the Diamond pack 0.75 ETH.

When will minting be available

The mint is now operational since a few minutes, we invite you to mint as soon as possible because of the growing hype thanks to the WCC community.

How many can we mint per wallet

You can mint up to 5 NFT per wallet to give everyone a chance to own one.

How can I use my nft?

You will be able to use your cyborgs in the metaverse soon. All the information will be available soon on our website and on the discord when it will welcome holders.

What is the metaverse?

The Metaverse refers to a shared virtual experience where land, avatars and names can be bought and sold, often using crypto-currencies. Future phases of the Wealth Cyborgs Club roadmap will allow you to join this Metaverse using the assets you own.
